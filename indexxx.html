Nasir Glass Industries Ltd. (NGIL)

Multi-functional float glass complex spread over 23 acres in Gazipur. NGIL operates a fully automated process conforming to Chinese and Japanese National Standards (GB11614-1999, J/S R3202), ensuring world-class quality in every sheet.

Key Products & Specifications

  • **Float Glass:** Clear and Tinted varieties.
  • **Specialized Sheets:** Tempered Sheets, Coated/Reflective Sheets, Mirrors.
  • **Thickness Range:** 3mm to 12mm.

Bangladesh Melamine Industries Ltd. (BMIL)

Product Focus: Bangladesh Melamine

  • **Tableware:** Oval dishes, Bowls, Plates, Tumbles, Mugs, and complete Gift sets.

Nasir Tobacco Industries Ltd (NTIL)

Commercial production started in 1997. NTIL ranks 4th in the country (as of 2013 data) and is a major exporter of cigarettes and tobacco components.

Nasir Leaf Tobacco Ind. Ltd. (NLTIL)

A dedicated grower, processor, packer, and exporter of different varieties of Bangladeshi Tobacco since 1998. The factory is fully equipped with automated American machinery.

N. Energy Saving Lamp Ind. Ltd. (NESLIL)

Manufactures high-quality energy-saving lamps. NESLIL was the first in Bangladesh to produce CFL products starting from basic components (glass tubes, phosphors, electronic components) via a partially automated process.

Products & Capacity

  • **Electric Lamps:** Spiral, Lotus, and U types.
  • **Wattage:** 5W to 65W.
  • **Capacity:** 40,000 pieces per day.
