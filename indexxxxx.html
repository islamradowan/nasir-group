Our Divisions

We are a diversified conglomerate with a strong presence in various key sectors of the economy.

Nasir Glass Industries Ltd. (NGIL)

Manufacturing high-quality float glass products; clear, tinted, tempered sheets.

Bangladesh Melamine Industries Ltd. (BMIL)

Pioneer in melamine tableware since 1985; export-quality dishes, bowls, mugs, and gift sets.

Nasir Printing & Packaging Ind. Ltd. (NPPIL)

Corrugated boards and carton-making for glassware, garments, food, and more.

Bangladesh Footwear Industries Ltd. (BFIL)

Quality EVA & PVC footwear for men, women and children. Brand: Jump Keds.

N. Energy Saving Lamp Ind. Ltd. (NESLIL)

Manufactures CFL & energy-saving lamps; capacity ~40,000 pcs/day.

N. Glassware & Tube Ind. Ltd. (NGTIL)

High-grade crystal white glass tableware and glass tubing for lamps.

Nasir Tobacco Industries Ltd. (NTIL)

Cigarette and tobacco production; notable export presence.

Nasir Leaf Tobacco Ind. Ltd. (NLTIL)

Grower, processor and exporter of Bangladeshi leaf tobacco varieties.

Nasir Biri Industries Ltd. (NBIL)

Established in 1977; manufactures handmade cigarettes with large domestic market.

Nasir Jute Industries Ltd. (NJIL)

Starting production of jute yarn/twine with large-scale capacity.
