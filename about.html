About Nasir Group of Industries

48 Years of Manufacturing Excellence Since 1977

Our Journey Through Time

1977

Foundation

Founded as Nasir Biri Industries in Kushtia by Late Nasir Uddin Biswas. Started as a regional biri manufacturer with a vision for industrial excellence.

1985

Melamine Pioneer

Launched Bangladesh Melamine Industries, becoming the pioneer melamine tableware manufacturer in Bangladesh with innovative production techniques.

1997

Footwear Revolution

Established Bangladesh Footwear Industries, launching the iconic Jump Keds brand that became Bangladesh's #1 footwear choice.

2005

Glass Manufacturing

Established first float glass factory (NGIL) in Gazipur, revolutionizing Bangladesh's glass industry with 40-60% market share.

2015

Industrial Diversification

Diversified into corn starch production and industrial products, expanding the group's manufacturing capabilities.

2018

Expansion Phase

Opened second float glass facility (NFGIL) in Tangail with 600 tonnes/day capacity, strengthening market leadership.

2025

Future Technologies

Launched Nasir Syntax Aluminium and Motors, entering advanced aluminium manufacturing and electric vehicle production for sustainable future.
