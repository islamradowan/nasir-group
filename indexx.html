Message from Chairman & MD

Nasir Uddin Biswas

Chairman & Managing Director

"Yesteryears' dreamers are today's doers."

At Nasir Group, we have built a legacy rooted in innovation, quality, and a deep commitment to contributing to our nation's economic growth. Our journey began with plastics and has expanded into diverse sectors including melamine tableware, footwear, float glass, and exciting new ventures in high-grade glass tableware and glass tubing.

Our vision extends beyond manufacturing excellence. We are dedicated to generating employment opportunities, fostering skilled workforce development, and ensuring that every product we create meets the highest standards of quality and reliability.

From our state-of-the-art float glass facilities to our pioneering melamine production, from our extensive footwear operations to our tobacco processing plants, each subsidiary represents our unwavering commitment to industrial excellence and national benefit.

We continue to invest in modern technology, skilled management, and strict quality control across all our operations, ensuring that Nasir Group remains at the forefront of Bangladesh's industrial landscape.

Our Industries

Explore our diverse portfolio of manufacturing excellence across multiple sectors

Nasir Glass Industries Ltd.

NGIL - Float Glass Manufacturing

Multi-functional float glass complex spread over 23 acres in Gazipur with fully automated processes conforming to Chinese and Japanese National Standards.

Products:

Float Glass (Clear & Tinted), Tempered Sheets, Coated/Reflective Sheets, Mirrors. Thickness: 3mm to 12mm.

Bangladesh Melamine Industries Ltd.

BMIL - Melamine Tableware

Pioneer in manufacturing melamine tableware since 1985, known for modern technology, skilled management, and strict quality control with global exports.

Products:

Melamine Tableware (Oval dishes, Bowls, Plates, Tumbles, Mugs, Gift sets). Brand: Bangladesh Melamine.

N. Energy Saving Lamp Ind. Ltd.

NESLIL - Energy Saving Lamps

First in Bangladesh to produce CFL products from basic components with partially automated processes, manufacturing high-quality energy-saving lamps.

Products:

Electric Lamps (Spiral, Lotus, U types). Wattage: 5W to 65W. Capacity: 40,000 pcs/day.

Bangladesh Footwear Industries Ltd.

BFIL - Footwear Manufacturing

One of the biggest footwear manufacturers since 1997, using professional skilled production units and high-quality EVA & PVC materials.

Products:

Footwear (Gents, Ladies & Kids shoes, keds, sandals). Brand: Jump Keds.

N. Glassware & Tube Ind. Ltd.

NGTIL - Glass Tableware & Tubing

Glass manufacturing unit using fully automated processes to produce high-grade, crystal white glass products for domestic and industrial use.

Products:

Glass Tableware (tumblers, cups, saucers, bowls) and Glass Tubing (for fluorescent & CFL lamps).

Nasir Tobacco Industries Ltd.

NTIL - Tobacco Manufacturing

Commercial production started in 1997, ranks 4th in the country, with extensive cigarette manufacturing and tobacco component exports.

Products:

Cigarettes (Brands: Nasir Gold, Asia, Top Ten), Cut Tobacco, CRES, Cork Tipping Paper, Filter Rods.

Nasir Leaf Tobacco Ind. Ltd.

NLTIL - Tobacco Leaf Processing

Grower, processor, packer, and exporter of different varieties of Bangladeshi Tobacco since 1998, equipped with automated American machinery.

Products:

Leaf Tobacco (Flue cured, Burley, Dark Virginia, Jati, Motihar) for local and international markets.

Nasir Printing & Packaging Ind. Ltd.

NPPIL - Packaging Solutions

Focuses on corrugated paper board making, multi-color printing, and carton making using high-quality imported raw materials.

Products:

Corrugated Paper Boards (2-ply, 3-ply, 5-ply), Carton Boxes for packaging (glassware, garments, tobacco, food, etc.).

Biswas Printing & Packaging Ind. Ltd.

BPPIL - Premium Packaging

Leading printing industry since 1977, specialized in export-quality corrugated paper board handling, multi-color printing, and carton making.

Products:

Carton making and multi-color printing for packaging (medicines, food, cosmetics, etc.).

Nasir Biri Industries Ltd.

NBIL - Handmade Cigarettes

Established in 1977, manufactures handmade cigarettes with a huge domestic market and employs over 10,000 workers.

Products:

Handmade Cigarettes (Brand: Nasir Biri).
